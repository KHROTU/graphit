## GRAPH CATEGORIES

1. **Mathematical Graphs** — Function plots, curves, transformations, coordinate geometry
2. **Statistical Charts** — Bar charts, histograms, pie charts, scatter plots, cumulative frequency, box plots
3. **Scientific Graphs** — Cooling/heating curves, velocity-time, distance-time, I-V characteristics, radioactive decay, reaction rates, population growth, enzyme kinetics
4. **Economic Graphs** — Supply/demand curves, PPC, cost/revenue curves, AD/AS, Phillips curve, Lorenz curve
5. **Geographic Graphs** — Climate graphs, population pyramids, hydrographs, beach profiles, cross-sections
6. **Business/Accounting Charts** — Break-even charts, cash flow diagrams, organizational charts, financial graphs
7. **Flowcharts/Diagrams** — Algorithms, system flowcharts, circuit diagrams, process diagrams
8. **Biological Diagrams** — Pyramids of biomass/energy/number, food webs, carbon/nitrogen/water cycles, predator-prey graphs, sigmoid growth curves
